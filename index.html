About Me

I am Niraj Kumar Sinha, a passionate educator and technology enthusiast with an M.Tech in Computer Science and an M.Sc. in Mathematics. My academic background has provided me with a strong foundation in both theoretical and applied aspects of computing, mathematics, and data-driven problem solving.

My areas of interest include Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, Computer Vision, and Software Development. I am committed to creating an engaging learning environment where students can develop strong technical skills, critical thinking abilities, and practical problem-solving expertise.

My goal is to contribute to academic excellence through quality teaching, research, innovation, and continuous learning while inspiring students to explore emerging technologies and pursue meaningful careers in Computer Science.
